# Company HR Policies

## 1. Remote Work and Work From Home (WFH)
Employees are allowed to work from home up to two days a week, typically on Tuesdays and Fridays. Core working hours are from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM, during which all remote employees must be available for meetings and communication. WFH requests must be submitted at least 24 hours in advance through the HR portal.

## 2. Sick Leaves
Each employee is entitled to 10 days of paid sick leave per calendar year. If an employee is sick for more than 3 consecutive days, a doctor's medical certificate must be provided to HR. Sick leaves cannot be carried forward to the next year and cannot be encashed.

## 3. Casual Leaves and Vacations
Employees are granted 15 days of paid casual leave (vacation) annually. Leaves must be approved by the reporting manager at least two weeks in advance for planned vacations. A maximum of 5 casual leaves can be carried forward to the following year. Unused casual leaves beyond 5 days will lapse.

## 4. Maternity and Paternity Leaves
Female employees are entitled to 26 weeks of paid maternity leave. Male employees are entitled to 2 weeks of paid paternity leave, which must be availed within the first two months of the child's birth. Both leaves require documentation from the hospital to be processed.

## 5. Office Dress Code
Our company follows a business casual dress code from Monday to Thursday. On Fridays, employees may dress in smart casuals, provided they do not have any client-facing meetings. Unacceptable attire includes ripped jeans, flip-flops, and offensive graphic t-shirts.
